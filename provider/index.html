Meet Your Provider

Clinical Experience. Personalized Care.

Marie-Claude Dubuc, PA-C brings more than 15 years of clinical experience to patient care. She has spent over a decade serving patients in South Florida emergency rooms, where she built a strong reputation for clinical judgment, calm leadership, and high standards of care.

She currently serves in a leadership role as Lead Physician Associate and brings hospital-level clinical experience into her weight management practice.

Patients value her clear communication, compassionate approach, and commitment to personalized care.

Education & Background

Education And Professional Foundation

Originally from Canada, Marie-Claude has lived in the United States for more than 30 years and is fluent in English and French, helping her communicate comfortably with a diverse patient population.

She earned her undergraduate degree from Hunter College in New York City, where she studied Biology and Psychology and completed a minor in Mathematics.

She later earned her Master of Medical Science from Nova Southeastern University in 2009 and became a Physician Associate.
