Questions & Answers
This FAQ provides general educational information only and does not replace a medical evaluation. Individual treatment plans and medication decisions are made by a licensed provider after review of your health history and clinical eligibility.

1. What does Weight Loss Davie offer?

Weight Loss Davie provides provider-led medical weight management services for adults seeking structured care, individualized planning, and ongoing clinical support.

2. Where are you located?

Weight Loss Davie is based in Davie, Florida in Broward County and serves patients in South Florida.

3. How do I get started?

You can contact the office directly or create an account on the registration page to begin the intake process and next-step scheduling.

4. Is care provider-led?

Yes. Care is delivered through a provider-led process that includes review of your history, treatment planning, patient education, and follow-up support.

5. Who is the provider?

Weight Loss Davie care is led by Marie-Claude Dubuc, PA-C, an experienced Physician Associate with extensive clinical experience in South Florida.

6. Who might be a candidate for medical weight management?

Candidacy depends on multiple factors, including your medical history, current health status, goals, and provider evaluation. A consultation is required to determine eligibility and appropriate options.

7. Are treatment recommendations the same for everyone?

No. Treatment planning is individualized. Recommendations depend on your health history, provider assessment, and clinical appropriateness.

8. Do I need a consultation before treatment?

Yes. A provider consultation is part of the process to review your health information, discuss goals, and determine safe and appropriate next steps.

9. Do you provide patient education and support?

Yes. Patient education and ongoing support are core parts of the Weight Loss Davie process, along with practical guidance and follow-through.

10. How long does the process take?

Timing varies by patient and depends on registration completion, intake information, scheduling availability, and provider review.

11. Do results vary by person?

Yes. Progress and outcomes vary based on individual factors, medical history, adherence to the plan, and provider recommendations.

12. Is ongoing follow-up part of care?

Yes. Ongoing follow-up supports monitoring, communication, and adjustments to the care plan when appropriate.

13. Do I need insurance to contact or start the process?

You can contact the office and begin the registration and intake process directly. For specific payment or administrative questions, contact the office for current details.

14. Can I ask questions before registering?

Yes. You can call or email the office with general questions before creating an account.

15. What should I have ready before my consultation?

Be prepared to provide accurate health history information and any requested records or documents. The office will guide you on what is needed for your appointment.

16. Do not include medical details in the contact form?

Correct. Do not submit sensitive medical details through the general contact form. Clinical information can be shared during your intake and provider consultation.

17. Can I register online?

Yes. Use the Register page to create your account and begin the intake process.

18. I already created an account. What now?

Use the Login page to access your account and continue with your intake steps or scheduling process.

19. How can I contact Weight Loss Davie?

Call 310.408.6687 or email contact@weightlossdavie.com.

20. Where can I learn more about your provider?

Visit the Provider page to learn more about Marie-Claude Dubuc, PA-C, her background, and the provider-led care approach.
